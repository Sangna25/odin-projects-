The Real Long Island Ice Tea

There are a few impostors out there that claim to be Long Island Iced Teas. In actuality, there is only one correct way to make a LIIT... and this is it!

The real and refreshing classic long island ice tea

Prep Time :5 mins

Cook Time :10mins

Total Time :15 mins

Servings :2 servings

What's In a Long Island Iced Tea?

  • ½ fluid ounce vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce rum
  • ½ fluid ounce gin
  • ½ fluid ounce tequila
  • ½ fluid ounce triple sec (orange-flavored liqueur)
  • 1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fluid ounce cola, or to taste
  • 1 lemon slice

Directions:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice; cover and shake.
  3. Pour cocktail into a Collins or hurricane glass; top with splash of cola for color. Garnish with a lemon slice.
  4. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves!
